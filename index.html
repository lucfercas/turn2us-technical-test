Understanding Your Welfare Benefits

What Does Universal Credit Cover?

Universal Credit is a financial support system for individuals and families with low income or those out of work. It helps with your daily living costs and can assist with housing, childcare expenses, and health-related costs. This single monthly payment is designed to simplify your benefit support, adapting as your circumstances change to ensure you always have the assistance you need.
